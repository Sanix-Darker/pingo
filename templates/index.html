Welcome to the Pingo !

The easiest way to track and follow your pings across the internet.

How does it work

First, you login, using github or google. Then you create a pingKey, it's attached with a link an will represent the amount of time that link got striked.

Why do you need pingo

This was for my personal use case, but i was frustrating to not knowing how often was my PRs/issues as a developer were either checked. And this is one of uses cases, let also say, you send a mail, inside the mail you provide a : if the email is opened (well with image accepted as preview), you're going to be notified about it, at least, only the amount of strike, your ping received.

What to expect from Pingo

Litterally nothing special, this is a side-project i did in a late night saturday and i wanted to learn htmx at the same time. It's self Hosted, so you can have your own instance of it.
